# Practical Upskilling Pathways for Perth Professionals

If you’re based in Perth and watching AI reshape the job market, here’s the good news: the bridge isn’t imaginary. It's already under construction.

These are your real, accessible pathways to join the transformation—not just spectate from the sidelines.

## 🎓 Entry-Level: TAFE WA & Foundations

**North Metropolitan TAFE** offers a low-cost ($30) Artificial Intelligence Skill Set designed to give you functional AI literacy through:

- Identifying AI applications  
- Automating tasks  
- Training machine learning models

Pre-reqs? Python and basic stats. It’s not for raw beginners, but it’s perfect for anyone who’s tinkered before.

## 🤝 Peer Learning + Meetups

**Perth AI Innovators** is a local community hosting events focused on:

- Use-case driven AI  
- Upskilling workshops  
- Real-world experimentation

It’s less “tech bros” and more builders & curious professionals trying to figure this stuff out—together.

## 🧠 Academic Depth: UWA + Online Blends

**UWA** offers formal AI degrees and short courses, with added focus on **ethics, policy, and applied implementation**. Great for those transitioning from other professions.

Prefer async learning?

- [Codecademy – Learn Python 3](https://www.codecademy.com/learn/learn-python-3) (23 hrs, beginner-friendly)  
- [Google AI – AI Essentials](https://grow.google/ai-essentials/)  
- [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/learn) – for when you’re past the basics

## 🛠 Toolsets to Focus On

If you’re retraining in Perth right now, these give you leverage fast:

- **Python** – the lingua franca of AI  
- **TensorFlow / PyTorch** – model building  
- **AWS SageMaker** – deployment at scale  
- **Prompt Engineering** – low-code power user lane

## 🚀 Takeaway

You don’t need a CS degree. You need context, a few powerful tools, and a willingness to unlearn.  
And Perth? It’s one of the few places where early AI adopters still get to define the standard.

So if you’ve ever said, “I wish I got in earlier”—this is earlier.
