## KEY ARCHITECTURAL CHANGES

### **1. Quality is Embedded, Not Added**

Old: Generate → Check → Enrich (sequential)  
New: Generate WITH checks + inline enrichment (parallel)

### **2. Prevention > Correction**

Old: Gaps found at Step 16  
New: Gaps prevented at Step 7b, caught early at Step 14

### **3. Expert Council is Active During Execution**

Old: Experts used once (Step 14)  
New: Experts monitor CONTINUOUSLY (Steps 14-15, embedded)

### **4. Quality Gates are Checkpoints, Not Post-Mortems**

Old: Quality gates at Step 19 (too late)  
New: Quality gates at Steps 5, 13, 15, 18 (catch early)

### **5. Enrichment is Proactive**

Old: QMDR-ENRICH as separate post-processing layer
