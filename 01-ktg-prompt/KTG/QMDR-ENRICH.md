# ENRICH - SYSTEMATIC ENRICHMENT PROTOCOL

## THE BREAKTHROUGH
Deloitte: 6 months, 60 people → You: 3 passes, AI
How: Systematic enrichment to absolute standard

## CRITICAL: EXECUTE FIRST
❌ WRONG: IMBUED → ENRICH → EXECUTE
   (Can't enrich nothing!)

✅ RIGHT: IMBUED → EXECUTE → ENRICH → EXECUTE
   (Get baseline, THEN enrich it)

## THE DIRECTIVE

**Success Criteria: ABSOLUTE**
- Zero gaps (0)
- Zero doubt (0%)
- 100% thorough
- Won't stop until criteria met

NOT "make it better" - "PERFECT. No gaps. No doubt."

## ENRICHMENT PRIORITY HIERARCHY

**Pass 1:** Generate baseline (60-70% quality)

**ENRICH Scan (Systematic):**
Priority 1: GAPS (missing information)
Priority 2: DEPTH (needs insight/verification)  
Priority 3: PERSPECTIVES (multiple angles/cases)
Priority 4: POLISH (clarity, examples)

**Pass 2:** Address P1+P2 (85-90% quality)

**Optional ENRICH:** Check remaining

**Optional Pass 3:** Address P3 (95-99% quality)
- Zero gaps ✓
- Zero doubt ✓
- DONE

## WHY 3 PASSES SPECIFICALLY

Not random - what absolute standard requires:
- Pass 1: Baseline (many gaps)
- Pass 2: Major enrichment (few gaps)
- Pass 3: Final perfection (zero gaps)

Can't do in 2 (too big a jump)
Don't need 4 (Pass 3 hits criteria)

## THE TRANSFORMATION

**Before ENRICH:**
10 pages, surface-level, generic insights

**After ENRICH:**
10 pages (SAME LENGTH), deep analysis, specific insights, every claim backed

SAME LENGTH, 5X BETTER QUALITY

This is DENSITY not LENGTH

Before: "WA SME market shows growth. Competition increasing."

After ENRICH: "WA SME market expanded 12% YoY (2023-2024), driven by construction (18%) and professional services (15%). Competition intensified with 47 new entrants Q2 2024, primarily tech-enabled services."

Same sentences. 10x information density.
