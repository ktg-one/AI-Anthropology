Through our new partnerships program, Pro subscribers will enjoy exclusive offers and discounts from top brands in travel, health & wellness, finance, and beyond. You can save on popular services and subscriptions or discover something new while potentially offsetting the cost of your monthly Perplexity Pro subscription.

We've partnered with leading brands across multiple categories. Here are some examples:

| **Partner** | **Product/service overview** | **Category** |

| Selfbook | Hotel booking | Lodging |
| Oura | Oura Ring is a smart ring wearable that tracks sleep, recovery, activity, and wellness using biometric sensors. | Wearables |
| Caliber | Caliber is a fitness coaching platform that offers personalized strength training, nutrition guidance, and habit tracking. | Fitness |
| Turbotax |  | Taxes Filing |

| Function | Function Health is a personalized health testing service that offers comprehensive lab tests for a wide range of biomarkers. | Biomarkers |
| Chase Home Lending |  | Home loans |

Pro perks will expire by May 2026.

Perplexity will also integrate premium content from these industry-leading data providers: **Statista, PitchBook, and Wiley**. This initiative will bring legal, healthcare, market research, and financial data previously restricted to high-cost enterprise contracts directly to users.

We will cite this data in three ways:

- **Free Users**: Free users will now have access to limited searches per month, allowing them to experience the power of our premium data integrations. This upgrade reflects our commitment to making high-quality information accessible to everyone.
- **Enterprise Pro Users:** Our enterprise clients will benefit from the most premium data searches while leveraging the Perplexity platform at work.
