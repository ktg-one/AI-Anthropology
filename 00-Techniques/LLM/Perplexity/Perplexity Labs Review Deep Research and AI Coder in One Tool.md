## What Sets Perplexity Labs Apart?

Perplexity Labs isn't just another AI search tool. It's designed to generate more advanced final presentations, files, images, mini apps, and interactive elements.

In many cases, it can even produce downloadable code for your projects. Some examples of what you can create with Perplexity Labs include:

- Flashcards for studying
- Presentation slides
- Sales automation tools
- Financial report analysis

One of the key features that distinguishes Perplexity Labs is its access to multiple data sources. Users can search the web, academic papers, social media, and financial data. There's also an option to attach files, giving the AI access to even more information.

## Perplexity Labs vs ChatGPT: AI Research Tool Comparison

When comparing Perplexity Labs to other [AI tools](https://nocode.mba/articles/ai-tools) like ChatGPT, several key differences emerge:

- Depth of research capabilities: Perplexity Labs can access multiple data sources simultaneously, potentially providing more comprehensive results.
- Data visualization and chart creation: Unlike ChatGPT, Perplexity Labs can generate custom charts and graphs to visualize data.
- Access to real-time information: Perplexity Labs can pull current data, making it more suitable for up-to-date analysis.
- Customization options: Users have more control over the sources and types of data used in their research.

## Interactive AI Dashboards and Stock Analysis with Perplexity Labs

To test the capabilities of Perplexity Labs, I asked it to create an in-depth document analyzing the latest Shopify stock 10K data. The results were impressive:

- Perplexity Labs generated a detailed report with custom charts and graphs.
- The analysis appeared to use more sources than a typical AI research tool would.
- The report included visual elements that enhanced the presentation of the data.

This feature could be particularly useful for creating research reports that require data visualization, something that traditional AI chatbots often struggle with.

## Is Perplexity Labs Worth the Upgrade?

After testing Perplexity Labs, here's my take on whether it's worth the $20 upgrade:

- For researchers, students, and professionals who need to create research papers or reports with charts and dashboards, Perplexity Labs is an excellent tool.
- The ability to create interactive dashboards with real-time data is a standout feature.
- For app development, specialized tools like [Lovable](https://www.nocode.mba/articles/lovable-ai-app-builder) or [FlutterFlow](https://www.nocode.mba/tracks/The-Beginner-Guide-to-FlutterFlow-) might still be better options.
- The depth of research capabilities and data visualization options make Perplexity Labs a strong contender in the AI research tool market.

Ultimately, whether Perplexity Labs is worth the upgrade depends on your specific needs. If you frequently create research reports or need to visualize complex data, the $20 investment could significantly enhance your workflow.

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### What is Perplexity Labs?

Perplexity Labs is a new feature from Perplexity AI that allows users to create advanced presentations, files, images, mini apps, and interactive elements using AI technology.

### How much does Perplexity Labs cost?

Perplexity Labs is available as part of the Perplexity Pro upgrade, which costs $20.

### Can Perplexity Labs replace specialized app development tools?

While Perplexity Labs can create basic app interfaces, it may not fully replace specialized tools for complex app development. Tools like Lovable or FlutterFlow might still be better for dedicated app creation.

### What types of data sources can Perplexity Labs access?

Perplexity Labs can access web data, academic papers, social media, financial data, and even attached files provided by the user.

### Is Perplexity Labs better than ChatGPT for research?

Perplexity Labs offers some advantages over ChatGPT for research, including access to multiple data sources, the ability to create custom charts and graphs, and access to real-time information.

### Who would benefit most from using Perplexity Labs?

Researchers, students, financial analysts, and business professionals who need to create visually appealing reports or presentations would benefit most from Perplexity Labs.
