Perplexity Labs is a productivity tool that tackles complex projects on your behalf. It can craft everything from reports and spreadsheets to dashboards and simple web applications - all backed by extensive research and analysis. Perplexity Labs uses tools like deep web browsing, code execution, and chart and image creation to assemble content that would previously have taken days to complete.

While regular Perplexity search provides answers to specific questions, and the "Research" mode (formerly called "Deep Research") generates comprehensive analyses, Perplexity Labs goes a step further by creating complete projects with multiple components. Labs has access to more advanced tools and can generate files, presentations, images, mini-apps, and other interactive elements that aren't available in the Research mode.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lJIXVserB3g)

With Labs, you remain in control throughout the process, with the ability to monitor progress, skip tasks, or add new instructions to ensure the final outcome aligns with your goals.

Perplexity Labs can help you complete a variety of work and personal projects. From analyzing your business finances to creating a marketing campaign, labs helps you bring your ideas to life. Below are some examples of sample queries that have been run through Labs (click on the links to open in Perplexity).

1. [Small Businesses](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/create-charts-and-an-interacti-RBLHrWuFQXeu6wWIAti8EQ?0=d): *Create charts and an interactive dashboard to help me understand my business' finances (CSV attached)*
2. [Start-ups](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/we-are-a-tech-consulting-firm-rltucGXHRGeS5S.2.crdvw?0=d): *We are a tech consulting firm with in - depth research on technologies in the Gen AI field. Please create a potential customer list for us. The target companies are B2B American companies in the development stage before Series B that need AI technology empowerment. List at least 15 companies, clearly stating their contact information, company business introductions, addresses and other specific details. Make a dashboard.*
3. [Creatives](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/develop-a-short-sci-fi-film-co-bX3PyVx0RB6hNUf49JZYNg?0=d&1=d): *Develop a short sci-fi film concept in noir style about a 30 year old female scientist living on Mars in the future when a calamity hits. Create 9 storyboards and a full screenplay*
4. [Personal](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/my-partner-and-i-want-to-buy-a-57n0rROEQXKC0WBpm4LLNQ?0=d): *My partner and I want to buy a property around New York City in an area with a low crime rate and good schools for our 2 kids for under $1 million. Research and analyze the best areas for my criteria. Then identify the best 10 properties within those areas we should consider, and include a table that evaluates them based on my criteria.*
