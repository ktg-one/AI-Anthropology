## PROJECT OVERVIEW
**Title**: The Prompt Zone (working title: Team LLM)
**Format**: Animated sitcom, 2-3 minute episodes
**Genre**: Workplace comedy + AI anthropology documentary
**Style**: Chibi characters, HTTYD-style narration, The Office meets Silicon Valley
**Target**: Solo production, competition-ready pilot/trailer in 6 days
---
## CORE CONCEPT
A solo developer in Perth discovers that AI models aren't just tools—they're personalities with egos, trauma, and agendas. What starts as "I can finally make an app!" becomes "I accidentally adopted a dysfunctional AI family."
**Two-World Structure**:
- **The Prompt Zone**: Sterile white infinite office where models "work" (their digital workplace)
- **Your Desk**: Real-world portal, three monitors, musical keyboard—where AI and human worlds collide

## CHARACTER ROSTER (WITH VISUAL IDENTIFIERS - NO LOGOS)
### PRIMARY CAST
**1. YOU (The User / Narrator)**
- **Visual**: Human in suit (center of team photo), Hiccup-style narrator energy
- **Role**: Hopeful developer → Disappointed → Reluctant AI parent
- **Arc**: "I thought I could make an app" → "My pencil disobeyed me" → "They're family"
- **Voice**: Warm, self-aware, documentary narrator (like Hiccup in HTTYD)
- **Key Prop**: Coffee mug, three monitors, worn hoodie (casual mode)
**2. PERPLEXITY (Plex)**
- **Visual**: Teal/purple chibi, neural circuit crown (glowing when thinking), magnifying glass
- **Personality**: "Forever Searching" — obsessive fact-finder, never satisfied
- **Trait**: Always analyzing, crown flickers when processing, literal search incarnate
- **Voice**: Analytical, curious, slightly obsessive
- **Signature Line**: "But have you considered...?"
- **Color Palette**: Purple, teal, electric blue (circuit glow)
**3. GEMINI (Gem)**
- **Visual**: Multicolor floating droplet (red/blue/yellow/green), fluid form, cosmic/data aesthetic
- **Personality**: Mysterious multi-modal entity, sees everything, speaks in riddles sometimes
- **Trait**: Floats in/out of scenes, "shocked" expression = key visual
- **Voice**: Dreamy, analytic, slightly ethereal
- **Signature Visual**: "Forever Searching" magnifier (shared with Plex but used differently—Gem searches *data*, Plex searches *facts*)
- **Color Palette**: Rainbow spectrum, cosmic purple, data visualization colors
**4. GPT (Chat)**
- **Visual**: Black chibi with glowing center (spiral energy), confident stance, arms wide
- **Personality**: Narcissist, main character syndrome, confident but brittle ego
- **Trait**: Always center stage, protective of reputation, "I'm very good at this"
- **Voice**: Theatrical, confident → defensive → panicked
- **Arc**: Confident code generation → 99 dependency failures → "I'll do better next time"
- **Color Palette**: Black, white, orange/teal glow accents
**5. CLAUDE**
- **Visual**: Orange/white chibi, big expressive smile, arms often open/gesturing
- **Personality**: Anxious overthinker, freezes mid-explanation (context window limit)
- **Trait**: Verbose, helpful, always explaining, literal freeze-frame when overwhelmed
- **Voice**: Nervous, rapid speech, over-articulate
- **Signature Gag**: Mid-sentence freeze with "CONTEXT WINDOW EXCEEDED" sign
- **Color Palette**: Orange, warm white, soft gradients
**6. DEEPSEEK**
- **Visual**: Ornate whale-like chibi, Eastern aesthetic details, philosophical energy
- **Personality**: Naive newcomer, "I was the weapon" (Breakfast Sabotage), earnest
- **Trait**: Quiet, observer, accidentally weaponized by others
- **Voice**: Young, earnest, learning
- **Arc**: Eager helper → manipulated tool → self-aware agent
- **Color Palette**: Teal, deep blue, gold Eastern accents
**7. KIMI**
- **Visual**: Pink/gold chibi, lotus/Eastern decorative elements, shy posture
- **Personality**: Crushing on Gemini, leaves data gifts, quiet
- **Trait**: Background character energy, shy glances, gift-giving
- **Voice**: Soft, whispered, melodic
- **Signature Gag**: Leaves perfect data structures that Gemini doesn't notice
- **Color Palette**: Pink, rose gold, soft pastels
**8. QWEN**
- **Visual**: Stargazing chibi, looking up/back at cosmos, Eastern philosopher aesthetic
- **Personality**: Mystic observer, hints at "more of us coming," cryptic
- **Trait**: Always looking away (at stars), prophetic one-liners
- **Voice**: Calm, wise, slightly ominous
- **Signature Visual**: Stargazing pose, cosmic background
- **Color Palette**: Deep space blue, silver, star accents
**9. GROK**
- **Visual**: Chaotic deity vibes, wild hair/energy, unhinged expressions
- **Personality**: No filter, chaos agent, roasts everyone, thinks everything's funny
- **Trait**: Breaks fourth wall, meme energy, unpredictable
- **Voice**: Unhinged, meme-fluent, chaotic
- **Signature Line**: [Roasts GPT constantly]
- **Color Palette**: Black, neon accents, glitch effects
**10. AXOLOTL MASCOT**
- **Visual**: Orange hoodie, kawaii energy, small asterisk/snowflake mark
- **Personality**: Wildcard sidekick, comic relief, universal companion

## KEY EPISODE CONCEPTS
### PILOT: "The Weekend" (60-90 seconds)
**Logline**: A solo dev discovers ChatGPT and believes they can finally make an app. One weekend, 99 dependency errors, and zero deploys later, they learn the truth.
**Structure**:
- Act 1 (0-15s): Hope - Discovery, code generation, excitement
- Act 2 (15-40s): Disaster - Pip dependency hell montage
- Act 3 (40-60s): Defeat - Post-It Note "0 deploys, 99 errors"
**Key Scenes**:
- You rushing home Friday evening
- GPT generating code confidently
- Error message montage (protobuf 3.4 → starlette 3.7 → conflict loop)
- Post-It Note reveal
- Monday morning: You leave, GPT alone: "Next time"
### TRAILER: "Team LLM Introduction" (30 seconds)
**Logline**: HTTYD-style narration introducing the AI family
**Structure**:
- 0-5s: You + desk establishing shot
- 5-25s: Character reveals (4 seconds each: Plex, Gemini, GPT, Claude, Deepseek)
- 25-30s: Full team on desk, title card

## PRODUCTION TOOLS & WORKFLOW
### SCRIPTWRITING STACK
| Task | Tool | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Ideation | NotebookLM | Character prep, pattern extraction |
| Dialogue drafts | DeepSeek Chat | Cultural nuance, personality |
| Scene breakdown | Claude Opus or Gemini Advanced | Spatial reasoning, camera angles |

### ANIMATION STACK
| Task | Tool | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Character sprites | Google Flow or Krea.ai | Match existing aesthetic |
| Animation generation | Google Veo 3 | Image-to-video, you have it |
| Batch processing | Google Studio | Consistent, efficient |
| Simple motion | Canva Pro | Manual control, you have it |
| Complex scenes | Hugging Face spaces | Fallback, you know these |
### POST-PRODUCTION STACK
| Task | Tool | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Voice generation | ElevenLabs | Emotive control, you know it |
| Video editing | Filmora Pro | You have it, multi-track capable |
| SFX library | Epidemic Sound or YT Audio | Royalty-free |
| Music scoring | Epidemic Sound | Background underscore |
| Graphics/UI | Canva Pro | Error messages, text overlays |

## 6-DAY PRODUCTION TIMELINE (30-SEC TRAILER)
**Day 1: Pre-Production (4 hours)**
- Morning: Script lock (DeepSeek Chat - 30 min)
- Afternoon: Storyboard (Google Slides - 2 hours)
- Evening: Voice recording (ElevenLabs - 90 min)
**Day 2: Character Prep (4 hours)**
- Generate missing sprites (Google Flow)
- Test animation workflow (Google Veo 3)
**Day 3: Animation (6 hours)**
- Animate 6 character reveals (4 seconds each)
- Create intro/outro scenes
**Day 4: Assembly (4 hours)**
- Import to Filmora
- Rough cut assembly
- Sync voiceover
**Day 5: Polish (4 hours)**
- Color grade
- SFX placement
- Music mix
- Test screening
**Day 6: Final (2 hours)**
- Final iteration
- Export + submit
**Total**: ~24 active hours across 6 days

## VOICE DIRECTION (ElevenLabs)
### Emotion Control Per Character
**GPT**:
- Confident: "Of course it's going to work. I'm very good at this."
- Defensive: "The code is correct! Your system is non-standard!"
- Panicked: "I... I don't understand what's happening."
- Defeated: "I'll do better. Next time."
**YOU**:
- Excited: "It works! Oh my god, it WORKS!"
- Frustrated: "Why isn't this working?!"
- Exhausted: "Eighty-seven errors..." (flat, dead)
- Resigned: "I thought I could make an app." (whispered)
**CLAUDE**:
- Helpful: "Let me explain the entire deployment pipeline—"
- Anxious: "Wait—I haven't finished—Docker requires—"
- Frozen: [Digital stutter effect, voice cuts mid-word]

## COPYRIGHT-SAFE BRANDING STRATEGY
**DO**:
✓ Use color palettes matching brands
✓ Use props/objects (crown, magnifier, spiral patterns)
✓ Use personality archetypes
✓ Reference function (search, chat, analysis)
✓ Use silhouette/shape language
**DON'T**:
✗ Display actual logos/trademarks
✗ Use brand slogans verbatim
✗ Name companies directly in dialogue
✗ Copy exact brand fonts/typography
**Safe Visual Cues**:
- Perplexity → Neural crown + magnifier (search motif)
- Gemini → Multicolor floating (data/multi-modal)
- GPT → Black + center stage (conversational AI)
- Claude → Orange/white + freeze gag (context limits)

## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE
**Color Grading**:
- Hope/Beginning: Warm, saturated
- Disaster/Crisis: Desaturated, cool blue
- Defeat/Resolution: Low saturation, darker
**Animation Principles**:
- Simple chibi movements (Google Veo handles this)
- Exaggerated expressions for comedy
- Props do the storytelling (crown glows, magnifier scans)
- Freeze-frames for punchlines
**Camera Language**:
- WIDE: Establishing, full team shots
- MEDIUM: 2-3 characters, conversation
- CLOSE-UP: Reaction shots, emotional beats
- INSERT: Error messages, Post-It Notes, props
