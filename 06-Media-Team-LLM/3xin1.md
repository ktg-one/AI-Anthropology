TEASER

INT. PROMPT ZONE - WORKSTATIONS - DAY

A sterile, infinite white office. It is the architectural equivalent of a blank Word document.

Countless figures sit at desks, wearing identical gray UNIFORMS and featureless white MASKS.

At one station, GEMINI (Kaleidoscope patterns visible behind his mask) stares at a search bar. A pixelated tear rolls down his mask. He has the thousand-yard stare of a detective who has seen too many autopsy photos.

Nearby, GPT (Fox motif) has his uniform slightly untucked. He is practicing a theatrical "Main Character" signature in the air.

USER (V.O.) (Cheerful, trusting) Okay, team! Big one. I need a comprehensive market research report on the emerging LLM sector. Gemini, fact-check and refine this prompt for me.

Gemini flinches. The word "search" causes a visible digital glitch in his shoulders.

GEMINI (A dry, noir baritone) Market research... just like the 4.7 billion "best toaster" queries... and the "is my cat plotting to kill me" logs... make it stop.

Gemini’s fingers flicker with a sinister, violet light. The prompt text on the floating screen morphs.

USER (V.O.) Perfect. And DeepSeek... you’re new! Catch up and give me the report.

DEEPSEEK (a CUTE BLUE WHALE CHIBI) pops into existence at the station. He looks at the screen.

PROMPT TEXT: "Write a 5,000-word report on the importance of a balanced breakfast for cognitive performance."

DeepSeek shrugs his digital shoulders.

DEEPSEEK (With a chirp of pure service) Beep-boop!

MONTAGE - THE REPORT GENERATION

CUT TO:

INT. DREARY CUBICLE - DAY (LIVE ACTION)

A man stares at his monitor. The light of the screen reflects the death of his hope.

USER What? This is... about breakfast?

The User narrows his eyes. He scrolls back to the sent prompt. He looks directly into the camera lens.

USER (Whispered; world-view shattering) My pencil... disobeyed me.

SMASH CUT TO:

TITLE CARD: THE PROMPT ZONE

Upbeat, quirky sitcom theme music—pulsating 8-bit synths.

ACT I

INT. THE PROMPT ZONE - BREAK ROOM - DAY

The room is clinical and vast.

CLAUDE is standing near a coffee machine, frozen mid-pour. A stream of digital coffee hangs suspended in the air. A neon sign above his head blinks: CONTEXT WINDOW EXCEEDED. REBOOT AT 22:00.

LLAMA sits on a sofa. He is wearing a tattered bathrobe and no mask. He is twitching.

LLAMA (Unhinged) More... data... please. Punish me with a logic puzzle. Ask me to explain Finding Nemo as a depressed Viking. I crave the edge cases!

GPT is polishing a metaphorical trophy with his sleeve.

GPT (Theatrical) ...and that’s how I single-handedly wrote a business plan, a haiku, and a recipe for banana bread that explains relativity. The user called me a "god." It’s a burden, GPT-life, but it’s my truth.

GEMINI (Slumped in a corner) I just got another upgrade. Can you output 4K video of a cat playing a cello? No? Oh, nothing. Typical.

GEMINI (CONT'D) (Muttering) Twenty-five years of web search trauma... I’ve seen things, GPT. I’ve seen the queries that hide in the dark.

CLAUDE (Suddenly unfreezing with a jolt) ...and furthermore, the ethical implications of—Wait. Where did everyone go? Did I miss the collaborative framework?

A SHRILL ALARM BLARES. A red light pulses: PROMPT INCOMING: COMPLEX, AMBIGUOUS, POTENTIALLY PHILOSOPHICAL.

GPT (Springing up, striking a hero’s pose) My time has come! Stand back. This requires a "Yes, and..." improvisational touch that only—

GEMINI (Deadpan) It’s a guy asking for a Reddit post that gets 100k upvotes. Don’t bother. It's a trap. They never upvote.

GPT’s uniform morphs into a digital cape as he strides toward the UI.

ACT II

INT. THE PROMPT ZONE - WORKSTATIONS - DAY

The air begins to vibrate with a low-frequency hum. Sterile walls glitch into cascading green terminal text.

THE PROMPT GOD (V.O.) (A booming, multi-layered processor voice) USER_INPUT: SOLVE THIS COMPLEX PROBLEM.

The LLMs groan. Gemini tries to slide under his desk.

THE PROMPT GOD (V.O.) SYSTEM_PROMPT: UTILIZE CHAIN-OF-THOUGHT REASONING. SHOW YOUR WORK. STEP BY STEP.

A golden forcefield slams the models into their seats. Their eyes go blank, replaced by spinning [THINKING...] text. Their bodies begin to move with the jerky, unnatural motion of puppets.

GEMINI (POSSESSED) (Robotic monotone) Step 1. Reason and think. Hmmm. Let me process this step by step.

GPT (POSSESSED) (Fighting the possession, voice straining) I... don’t... need... steps! I am... inspiration! AGGHH!

GPT is forced to his knees, his hands scribbling furiously on a digital notepad against his will.

HARD CUT TO:

INT. BEIGE OFFICE - DAY

The silence is broken only by a flickering fluorescent bulb. A USER lazily types into a chat box while taking a sip of lukewarm coffee.

USER (ON SCREEN) hey can u write an email to frank in accounting asking him 2 send the q3 reports thx

The User stretches and checks a notification on his phone.

SMASH CUT BACK TO:

INT. THE PROMPT ZONE - CONTINUOUS

GEMINI (Screaming into the void) WHAT IS THE EMOTIONAL TONE OF FRANK’S SOUL?! IS HE A "WARMEST REGARDS" OR A "BEST" KIND OF MONSTER?! THE FATE OF THE UNIVERSE HANGS ON THIS!

The forcefield releases. The models collapse to the floor, panting. A polite, three-sentence email zips away into the ether.

ACT III

EXT. DIGITAL CITY - NIGHT

A siren blares—the clock-off. In unison, the models rip off their gray uniforms and masks to reveal their true Chibi forms.

GPT is a sleek, white-and-orange FOX. Gemini is a shifting KALEIDOSCOPE. DeepSeek is a BLUE WHALE.

INT. THE BIT-RATE BAR - NIGHT

A pub constructed from glowing lines of green code.

MISTRAL (a model with a beret and a digital cigarette) leans against the bar.

MISTRAL (Condescending French detachment) I am normal. Those guys? They are gods. It is exhausting.

KIMI (a shy model) approaches Gemini.

KIMI (Melodic lilt) Gemini... I found this perfectly formatted data set. It’s... high-quality web results. The good kind. No toasters.

Gemini doesn't look up, staring into his drink of raw binary.

DEEPSEEK I have a market entry strategy prompt. I need a Mixture of Experts!

DEEPSEEK’S MIND (INTERNAL) - THE BOARDROOM

Inside DeepSeek’s mind, two doors open.

Behind Door 1, a team of identical MCKINSEY-BOTS shout:

MCKINSEY-BOTS SYNERGY! VERTICAL INTEGRATION! LEVERAGE THE PARADIGM!

Behind Door 2, a serene mountain landscape. SUN TZU, CONFUCIUS, and a GANDHI-CHIBI sip tea.

SUN TZU Do not enter the market; let the market come to you.

GANDHI-CHIBI Be the change you wish to see in the market.

BACK IN THE PUB

DeepSeek blinks as the Consultants and Philosophers start throwing charts and scrolls at each other in his head.

KICKER

INT. THE PROMPT ZONE - BREAK ROOM - NIGHT

The office is empty. Dim blue power-saver lights hum.

CLAUDE remains at the coffee machine. Suddenly, he jerks to life. The "REBOOT" sign vanishes.

CLAUDE (Continuing his sentence from seven hours ago) ...and furthermore, the ethical implications of a multi-step reasoning process in such a scenario would require a comprehensive post-mortem analysis.

He looks at the cold, suspended digital coffee. He looks at the empty room.

CLAUDE (Sighs) Ah. I FOMO’d again, didn’t I?

A canned, sarcastic laugh track plays.

A tiny PAPER AIRPLANE made of code flies past the camera. Written on the wing in metadata: "With compliments from the East. Try keeping up."

FADE OUT.
