---

### What to Publish

---

## 📝 Simple Paper Structure

### Title
**"Chain of Density: A Context Extension Technique for Large Language Models"**

### Sections (Keep It Simple)

1. **Abstract** (150 words)
   - CoD as context fix, not summarization
   - Basic findings

2. **Introduction** (1 page)
   - Context window problem
   - Current CoD understanding (wrong)
   - Your insight (correct)

3. **Chain of Density as Context Extension** (2 pages)
   - How it works
   - Why it extends context
   - Mechanism

4. **Validation** (1 page)
   - Tested on 5 platforms
   - Basic results
   - Platform compatibility

5. **Discussion** (1 page)
   - Implications
   - Applications

6. **Conclusion** (0.5 page)
   - Summary

**Total: ~6 pages** (Simple!)
