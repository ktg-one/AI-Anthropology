## 🔒 Safety Checklist

### Before Using Any Platform

**Check:**
- [ ] Privacy policy
- [ ] Terms of service
- [ ] Data retention policy
- [ ] Who can access your prompts
- [ ] Can they use your prompts for training?
- [ ] Export capabilities
- [ ] Delete capabilities

### Red Flags
- ❌ No privacy policy
- ❌ Vague ToS
- ❌ Can't export data
- ❌ Can't delete data
- ❌ Prompts used for training (without consent)
