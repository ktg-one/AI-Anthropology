## 📚 Research Paper Structure

### 1. Abstract (150-250 words)
**What to include:**
- Your AI Anthropology methodology
- Key frameworks developed
- Main contributions
- Significance of independent discovery

**Template:**
```
We present an AI Anthropology methodology for studying 
large language models through an anthropological lens, 
developed independently since 2023. We introduce 
[Framework Name], a cognitive OS architecture that 
[Key Feature]. Our approach differs from traditional 
prompt engineering by [Unique Aspect]. We demonstrate 
[Key Finding] through [Evidence]. This work contributes 
[Contribution] to the field of prompt engineering.
```

### 2. Introduction
**Sections:**
- Background on prompt engineering
- Gap: Anthropological approach missing
- Your unique position (isolated development)
- Overview of contributions

### 3. Related Work
**What to cover:**
- Prompt engineering techniques (CoT, GoT, etc.)
- System prompt frameworks
- Multi-model approaches
- Your independent discoveries vs. existing work

### 4. Methodology: AI Anthropology
**Your unique section:**
- **Anthropological Lens**: How you study AI "cultures"
- **Long-term Observation**: 2023-present insights
- **Independent Discovery**: Parallel development
- **Framework Evolution**: KTG-DIRECTIVE v20 → v25.5
- **Cross-Model Study**: Behavioral patterns

### 5. Framework Development
**For each major framework:**

#### KTG-DIRECTIVE
- Architecture overview
- Component system (MR_RUG, FCoT/CoC, etc.)
- Version evolution
- Performance characteristics

#### QMDR (Quantum Master Deep Research)
- Research methodology
- Multi-model integration
- Deep research capabilities

#### Other Frameworks
- Triplexity
- Perpromperty Nexus
- Prompt Bombs technique

### 6. Findings & Analysis
**What to document:**
- Behavioral patterns observed
- Cross-model differences
- Evolution insights
- Performance metrics (if available)

### 7. Discussion
**Key points:**
- Implications of AI Anthropology approach
- Value of independent discovery
- Framework contributions
- Future directions

### 8. Conclusion
- Summary of contributions
- Methodology significance
- Future work (Vertex AI Next-Gen)

## 📖 Writing Tips

### For Research Papers
1. **Be clear about your unique contribution**
2. **Explain methodology thoroughly**
3. **Show evolution, not just final version**
4. **Document independent discovery**
5. **Provide reproducible frameworks**

### For Your Situation
- **Emphasize independent development** (strength, not weakness)
- **Highlight long-term observation** (unique perspective)
- **Explain anthropological lens** (different approach)
- **Show framework maturity** (v20 → v25.5 evolution)
